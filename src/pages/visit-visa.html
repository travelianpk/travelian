Visit Visa FAQs

What documents are required for a visit visa?

Required documents typically include a valid passport (with at least 6 months validity), passport-size photographs, bank statements, flight bookings, hotel reservations, travel insurance, and supporting documents like employment letter or invitation letter. Requirements vary by country.

How long does it take to process a visit visa?

Processing times vary by country. Tourist visas typically take 5-15 working days, though some countries offer express processing. We'll provide you with the estimated timeline for your chosen destination and help expedite the process.

What is the validity period of a visit visa?

Visit visa validity varies by country and visa type. Most tourist visas are valid for 30-90 days, with some countries offering multiple-entry visas valid for 6 months to 5 years. We'll guide you on the best visa option for your travel plans.

Can you help with visa rejection cases?

Yes, we can review your previous rejection, identify the reasons, and help you reapply with a stronger application. We provide expert guidance on documentation and interview preparation to improve your chances of approval.

Do I need travel insurance for a visit visa?

Many countries require travel insurance as a mandatory document for visa applications. We can assist you in obtaining comprehensive travel insurance that meets the requirements of your destination country.

Can I extend my visit visa while abroad?

Extension policies vary by country. Some countries allow visa extensions from within their territory, while others require you to exit and reapply. Contact us for specific information about your destination country's extension policies.

What are your service charges for visa processing?

Our service fees vary depending on the destination country, visa type, and processing speed. The prices shown include our service charges. Contact us via WhatsApp or phone for a detailed breakdown and any current promotions.

Do you provide assistance with hotel and flight bookings?

Yes! We provide complete travel solutions including flight tickets, hotel reservations, travel insurance, and airport transfers. Our packages can be customized to include accommodation and transportation for your convenience.

What payment methods do you accept for visa services?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, cash payments at our office, and online payments. Flexible payment options are available. Contact us via WhatsApp or visit our office for payment details and arrangements.
