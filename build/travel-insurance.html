Information Required for Travel Insurance

To get a quote and issue your policy, we need the following details. Share them via WhatsApp and we’ll send you the best options.

Personal details

  • Full name (as on passport)
  • Date of birth
  • Nationality
  • Passport number
  • Contact number & email

Trip details

  • Destination country/countries
  • Departure date
  • Return date
  • Purpose of travel (holiday, Umrah, business, etc.)

Coverage preferences

  • Type: Single trip or annual multi-trip
  • Sum insured (if you have a preference)
  • Number of travellers (individual / family)

Health & other

  • Any pre-existing medical conditions (if applicable)
  • Special requirements (e.g. adventure activities)

Travel Insurance Guide & FAQs

Travel insurance provides protection against unforeseen events during your trip, including medical emergencies, loss of passport or luggage, delayed departure, trip cancellation, and more. Many countries (e.g. Schengen) require it for visa approval.
Travel insurance typically covers medical emergencies abroad, trip cancellation or interruption, lost or delayed baggage, and 24/7 travel assistance. Coverage varies by plan and destination – we’ll recommend the right option for your trip.
