TEAM PHOTOS FOLDER
==================

Place your team member photos in this folder using these exact filenames:

  member-1.jpg  (or .jpeg, .png) - Team Member 1 / CEO
  member-2.jpg  (or .png) - Team Member 2 / Visa & Tours Specialist
  member-3.jpg  (or .png) - Team Member 3 / Operations Manager
  member-4.jpg  (or .png) - Team Member 4 / Customer Support

Supported formats: .jpg, .jpeg, .png

If an image is missing, a placeholder icon will be shown instead.
