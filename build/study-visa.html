Study Visa FAQs

Which countries does Travelian.pk provide study visa services for?

Travelian.pk provides complete study visa assistance for the United Kingdom, Finland, Sweden, Hungary, Australia, Austria, Germany, Netherlands, and Greece.

Does Travelian.pk assist with university selection and admissions?

Yes. Travelian.pk assists students with university and course selection, application submission, and offer letter follow-up based on academic profiles and career goals.

Does Travelian.pk help with SOP, personal statement, and documentation?

Yes. Travelian.pk provides complete guidance for SOPs, personal statements, and all required visa documentation according to university and embassy requirements.

Does Travelian.pk guarantee a study visa?

No consultancy can legally guarantee a visa. Travelian.pk follows a transparent, honest, and well-structured process to maximize approval chances.

How can the study visa process be started with Travelian.pk?

The process can be started by sharing details through the website or contacting Travelian.pk via call or WhatsApp, after which experts reach out for guidance.

What documents are required for a study visa application?

Required documents typically include academic transcripts and certificates, a valid passport, offer letter from the university, SOP or personal statement, financial documents, and English language test results. Exact requirements vary by country and university, and Travelian.pk provides complete guidance based on each case.
